# 🚀 Supplier Evaluation System v2.0 - Quick Start Guide

## 📍 Access Point

**Customer Portal** → Menu: **"Supplier Evaluation"** (🎯 Target icon)

## ⚡ 3-Minute Quick Start

### Step 1: Load Your Quotation (30 seconds)
1. Select a quotation from the dropdown
2. System auto-loads:
   - Total quantity
   - Regional duty/VAT rates
   - Base supplier (Fujian Gaoshengdafu)

### Step 2: Add Competitors (1 minute)
1. Click **"Add Competitor"** button
2. Enter competitor FOB prices
3. Fill in capability metrics (or use defaults)

### Step 3: Evaluate (1.5 minutes)
1. **STEP 1**: Review capability scores → See who qualifies (≥60 pts)
2. **STEP 2**: Check TCO rankings → See true total cost
3. **STEP 3**: Get AI recommendation → Make decision

## 🎯 Customer Type Selection

**Choose your profile before STEP 3:**

| Type | When to Use | Weight Distribution |
|------|-------------|---------------------|
| 💰 **Price-Sensitive** | Tight budget, price-competitive market | Price 70% + Capability 30% |
| ⭐ **Quality-First** | Brand reputation critical, premium market | Capability 60% + Price 40% |
| ⚖️ **Balanced** | Experienced buyer, TCO-focused | Capability 50% + Price 50% |

## 📊 Understanding the Scores

### Capability Score (STEP 1)
- **85-100**: A级 ✅ Strategic Partner
- **70-84**: B级 ✅ Qualified Supplier
- **60-69**: C级 ⚠️ Trial Period
- **<60**: D级 ❌ Not Qualified (Excluded from STEP 2)

### TCO Ranking (STEP 2)
- Includes FOB + Freight + Duty + **Hidden Costs**
- Hidden costs based on quality/delivery risks
- Lower TCO = Better value

### Final Recommendation (STEP 3)
- 🏆 **Highly Recommended**: Score ≥80, Top capability + Good price
- ✅ **Recommended**: Score ≥65, Solid choice
- ⚠️ **Acceptable**: Score ≥50, Proceed with caution
- 🚨 **Not Recommended**: Score <50, High risk

## 💡 Pro Tips

### For Price-Sensitive Customers
- Focus on **STEP 2 TCO rankings**
- Don't just look at FOB price
- Check hidden cost breakdown
- Best Value = High capability + Low TCO

### For Quality-First Customers
- Prioritize **STEP 1 capability scores**
- Look for A/B grade suppliers
- Check quality metrics carefully
- Premium pricing acceptable for top performers

### For Balanced Customers
- Use **STEP 3 quadrant map**
- Target upper-right quadrant (Best Value)
- Avoid lower-left quadrant (Poor Value)
- Balance capability and cost equally

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Don't Do This
1. **Skipping STEP 1**: Going straight to price comparison
2. **Ignoring hidden costs**: Only looking at FOB price
3. **Wrong customer type**: Using price-sensitive when quality matters
4. **Accepting D-grade suppliers**: Even if cheapest

### ✅ Do This Instead
1. **Complete all 3 steps**: Follow the framework
2. **Review TCO breakdown**: Understand total cost
3. **Match customer type to reality**: Be honest about priorities
4. **Only choose qualified suppliers**: B-grade minimum for critical items

## 🔄 Real-World Example

### Scenario: Customer needs 1,000 units

**Quotations Received**:
- Supplier A: $10.00 FOB
- Supplier B: $9.00 FOB
- Supplier C: $8.50 FOB

**Without Evaluation System**:
- ❌ Choose C (cheapest)
- Result: Quality issues, late delivery, total disaster

**With Evaluation System**:

**STEP 1 Results**:
- A: 91 pts (A级) ✅
- B: 78 pts (B级) ✅
- C: 57 pts (D级) ❌ EXCLUDED

**STEP 2 Results** (only A & B):
- A: $12,300 TCO
- B: $11,800 TCO ← Lower!

**STEP 3 Result** (Price-Sensitive Type):
- B: Weighted Score 82.5 → 🏆 **Highly Recommended**

**Final Decision**: Choose Supplier B
- ✅ Qualified (78 pts)
- ✅ Lowest TCO ($11,800)
- ✅ Best value for price-sensitive customers
- ✅ Avoided disaster with Supplier C

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Problem: "No qualified suppliers"
**Solution**: All suppliers scored <60
- Review capability metrics (too strict?)
- Consider lower-grade suppliers for non-critical items
- Expand supplier search

### Problem: "TCO too high across all suppliers"
**Solution**: 
- Negotiate better payment terms (+10 pts)
- Request MOQ flexibility (+3 pts)
- Improve communication speed (+8 pts)

### Problem: "Can't decide between A and B grade"
**Solution**:
- Check STEP 3 quadrant position
- Review hidden cost breakdown
- Consider customer type weights
- Trust AI recommendation
